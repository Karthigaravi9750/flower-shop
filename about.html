about flower:

Flowers carry importance in each nook and corner of the world. They also come in use for making medicines. Similarly, we also make difference in fragrance perfumes from the flowers. Further, the butterflies, birds and bees take the flowers as food.

Flower:

flower, Reproductive portion of any flowering plant (angiosperm). Popularly, the term applies especially when part or all of the reproductive structure is distinctive in colour and form. Flowers present a multitude of combinations of colour, size, form, and anatomical arrangement.

Weird Facts About Flowers:

Some flowers are carnivorous and trap insects to digest them.

Flowers can hear buzzing bees.

Some flowers are used as natural insecticides. ...

Flowers are thermogenic and can generate heat to attract pollinators.

There are over 300,000 different species of flowering plants in the world!
